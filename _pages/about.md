As of 2023 I am a PhD student in Mathematics at the [National & Kapodistrian University of Athens](https://www.math.uoa.gr/), under the supervision of Professor [Aristides Kontogeorgis](http://users.uoa.gr/~kontogar/). I am from Greece and I have received a B.A. and M.A. in pure mathematics from the same university.

I will be working on Number Theory and more specifically in Arithmetic Topology about the analogue between prime numbers and knots. I am also interested in Galois Representations and Cryptography.

In my free time, I hit the gym or I play [chess](https://ratings.fide.com/profile/42173477). Feel free to contact me!
